The Project

Hapyak has set out to make the world of video alot more interactive. They want to empower people to experience video on the web more like they do in person using links in video, questions, chapters, ability to comment and draw within videos uploaded from the web. Users can finally engage with video on the web.

My Role

I was hired in by the CEO to work on a better user experience and give the web application a new clean design. I also worked with the developers on simplifying the new video interface.

The Design Challenges

To take the current branding and create new layouts that were to be responsive and work on all devices. The video interface has many components that needed to be streamlined on top of the users video. They were also adding some new white-label product offerings which needed to be incorporated into the video design.
